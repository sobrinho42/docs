PolyTracker features a detailed notification system. The goal isn't just to inform you *what* happened, but to provide context regarding the *conviction* and the outcome of that trade.

There are **6 types of alerts**. Below, we explain the structure of each one so you can read the market in seconds.

---

## 1. New Position

This alert triggers when the investor enters a market where they previously had no open position. It is the start of a trade.

<Frame caption="Example: New Position Alert">
  <img src="/images/notif-new-position.png" alt="New Position Alert" />
</Frame>

**Understanding the data:**
* **Wallet:** The name/label of the wallet taking action.
* **Market & Outcome:** The event and the specific outcome (e.g., "Trump Winner", "Yes").
* **Shares:** Number of shares purchased.
* **Position Details:**
  * `Avg Price`: The average price paid per share.
  * `Value`: The total amount invested in Dollars.
  * `(% Portfolio)`: **Critical Metric.** Shows how much this trade represents of the investor's total portfolio/bankroll.
* **Time:** Exact time of the trade.

---

## 2. Position Updated: BUY (Increasing Position)

Occurs when the investor was already positioned and decides to **buy more**. This usually signals increased confidence in the thesis or a move to average down their entry price (DCA).

<Frame caption="Example: Position Increase">
  <img src="/images/notif-update-buy.png" alt="Update Buy Alert" />
</Frame>

**What changes here:**
* **Shares:** Shows the change in share count with an indicator.
  * *Example:* `10,000 -> 15,000 (+5,000)`.
* **Buy Details:** Shows the average price and value of *this specific new purchase*.
* **Total Position:** The accumulated summary of the entire position after this buy.
* **Open PnL:** Profit and Loss if the investor were to close the trade right now.

---

## 3. Position Updated: SELL (Partial Exit)

Occurs when the investor sells only **a portion** of their shares. They might be taking profit ("money in the pocket") or reducing risk exposure.

<Frame caption="Example: Partial Sell">
  <img src="/images/notif-update-sell.png" alt="Update Sell Alert" />
</Frame>

**What changes here:**
* **Shares:** Shows the reduction in shares.
  * *Example:* `15,000 -> 10,000 (-5,000)`.
* **Sell Details:** The price and value of this specific sale.
* **Realized PnL:** The consolidated Profit or Loss referring *only to this sold portion*.
* **Position Left:** How much skin they still have in the game and the open PnL of that remainder.

---

## 4. Position Closed: SOLD (Full Exit)

The investor closed the position completely **before** the event was concluded by Polymarket. They chose to exit the market voluntarily.

**Understanding the data:**
* **Exit Details:** Compares the `Entry Price` vs. `Exit Price`.

## 5. Position Closed: REDEEM (Market Settlement)

These alerts occur when the event comes to an end and Polymarket settles the positions. There is no more trading here, just the final result.
