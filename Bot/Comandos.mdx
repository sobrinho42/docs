The Main Menu is your command center. It was designed to be streamlined, allowing you to add new wallets, manage your alerts, and configure the bot with just a few clicks.

Below, we detail the function of each button in the order you will likely use them.

---

## 1. Track New Wallet

This is where you start monitoring an investor. To do this, you will need their **public wallet address**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the Address">
    On the investor's profile within Polymarket, locate and copy the wallet address (usually starting with `0x...`).
    
    <Frame caption="Where to find the address on a Polymarket profile">
      <img src="/images/polymarket-copy-address.png" alt="Copying the address" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register in the Bot">
    Click the **Track New Wallet** button and, when prompted, paste and send the address you just copied.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a Label (Nickname)">
    Give this wallet a name to identify it (e.g., "Trump Whale", "Sports Sniper"). This name will appear in all future notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## 2. Manage Wallets

This is your management hub. Clicking here displays the list of all wallets you are currently following.

By clicking **Settings** next to a specific wallet, you open that investor's **Individual Panel**, giving you access to control tools:

<Frame caption="Individual Wallet Configuration Panel">
  <img src="/images/bot-wallet-settings.png" alt="Wallet Settings" />
</Frame>

### Individual Management Tools

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Positions (Snapshot)" icon="chart-pie">
    Instantly shows the investor's **top open positions** right now. Ideal for quickly understanding where their money is allocated without waiting for a new notification.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Pause / Resume" icon="pause">
    Want to temporarily silence a trader without unfollowing? Use this button to pause and resume alerts for this specific wallet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Value & % Threshold (Noise Filters)" icon="filter">
    **Crucial for your strategy.** Configure triggers to be notified only on relevant moves:
    
    * **Value Threshold:** Notify only trades above $X (e.g., above $500).
    * **% Threshold:** Notify only if the trade represents more than Y% of their portfolio (e.g., above 5% of their bankroll).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Rename & Untrack" icon="pen-to-square">
    * **Rename:** Change the wallet's label.
    * **Untrack Wallet:** Stop following the wallet and remove it from your list.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 3. General Settings

Here you define the global preferences for your bot, which apply to all notifications.

### Notification Mode
Defines how the chat history behaves.

| Mode | How it works | Recommendation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Clean Mode** | Old messages from the same market are **deleted** and replaced by the latest update. Keeps the chat clean. | âœ… **Recommended** |
| **History Mode** | Generates a **new message** for every update. No message is deleted, creating a full historical log. | **Audit Trail** |

### Other Adjustments
* **Change Timezone:** Adjust your timezone so that alerts display the correct local time for your region.
* **Change Language:** Switch the bot language (Portuguese/English).
