## Anatomy of an Analysis

Every post on Radar is direct and objective, delivering only the data necessary for your decision-making.

You will find:
1.  **Context:** The wallet's focus, style, and "personality".
2.  **Performance:** Overview of profits and consistency.
3.  **X-Ray:** Strengths and watchouts (risk factors).
4.  **Direct Access:** Links to the Polymarket profile and external Analytics tools.
